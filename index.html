Overview

This study asks participants to complete a brief text-coding task across six days. Each day shows rows one at a time; each row requires a label and a short evidence note. Some participants will have AI assistance or shortened workloads depending on random assignment.

1 • Consent

Start in Qualtrics and be randomized to a condition.

2 • Start Day

Click your Day link below to open the task and begin.

3 • Finish & Return

Finish the task and return tomorrow. We track progress for bonuses.

How it works

Use the same browser & device when possible. Your session is validated by a secure token passed in the link. The task app records start/end times, per-row times, and whether you leave the tab. We do not record your clipboard contents (only paste attempts).

Daily Tasks

Click the day you want to complete. Links include a session token that the server validates.
